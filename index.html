Who We Are

The story of Aavien International begins with a legacy rooted in excellence as Indentor & Manufacturer Representative (OEM)


Our journey started in 1967 when our parent company was founded in India with a strong focus on delivering high-quality solutions to the Nationalised Industries, mainly Energy, Railway Rolling Stock & Marine sector. Over the decades, the company built a solid reputation through deep technical expertise, long-term partnerships, and a commitment to solving real industrial challenges.


With the vision to expand globally and contribute to the growing industrial landscape of the Middle East, Aavien International Trading LLC SPC was established in Abu Dhabi, UAE. As a proud sister company of Energy and Power DMCC, we continue to deliver advanced chemical solutions that reduce carbon footprint and enhance fuel performance for energy producers and industrial facilities.

We're proud to be a sister company of Energy and Power DMCC and hold an ICV Score of 44.86%, demonstrating our strong local contribution

Mission

To foster trusted global partnerships that drive sustainable industrial growth and reduce the carbon footprint, by leveraging our deep expertise in energy, marine & railway solutions.

Vision

To be a leading force in UAE’s infrastructure development, pioneering efficient and sustainable solutions across different sectors, and empowering regional prosperity.
